Schönbrunn

Austrian

You can go in but I would walk up the hill and see the view from the top, maybe have a coffee in the beautiful café the Glorietta. The worlds oldest Zoo is in Schönbrunn which was once a private menagerie of emperor Franz Stephan and Maria Theresa.
