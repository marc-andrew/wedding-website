Coffee Places

The coffee culture played an important part in Viennese history and is part of our heritage. In 2011 the Viennese coffe houses were put on the UNESCO’s Intangible Cultural Heritages. Viennese people drink Mélange – you should try one at one of the below cafes.

Fun fact: Cappuccinos first popped up as the 'Kapuziner' in Viennese coffee houses in the 1700s. A description of the 'Kapuziner' from 1805 described it as "coffee with cream and sugar", and a description of the drink from 1850 adds "spices" to the recipe. Either way, these drinks had a brown color similar to the robes worn by the Capuchin ('Kapuzin') friars in Vienna, and this is where their name came from.
