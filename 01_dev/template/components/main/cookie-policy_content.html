Cookie Policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of information which are issued to your computer when you visit our website and which store and sometimes track information about your use of our site.

Why do we use cookies?

We use cookies to elevate your user experience and the quality of our site. It help us improve the site by measuring any errors which occur during your browsing experience.
